\section{Evaluate the Poisson's ratio}
Difficulty = easy
Number Points = 2
\subsection{Problem}
Evaluate the Poisson's ratio for steel with a Young's modulus of $212.5$ GPa(1) and shear modulus of $77.05$ GPa(1)
\subsection{Solution}
The Poisson's ratio is $E/(2G) - 1$ = 0.379.
